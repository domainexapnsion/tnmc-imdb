Plot Summary

Set in the heart of Mumbai, Topiwala National Medical College follows a rotating cast of idealistic students as they navigate academic pressure, hierarchy, sleep deprivation, ethical dilemmas, and moments of quiet triumph. What begins as a dream slowly evolves into a test of resilience, character, and purpose.
